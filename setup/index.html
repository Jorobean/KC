KEMS Mirror Setup

Get your Mirror connected to WiFi in 3 simple steps

Welcome to KEMS Mirror!

This guide will help you connect your Mirror device to your home WiFi network.

Step 1 Connect to the Hotspot

On your phone or tablet:

  1. Look for a WiFi network called "Mirror-Setup"
  2. Connect to it (password: MirrorSetup123)
  3. Wait for connection confirmation
üí° Tip: If you don't see the network, your Mirror is still starting up. Wait 30-60 seconds and try again.

Step 2 Open the Setup Interface

Once connected to Mirror-Setup, click the button below to access the setup interface:

üì° Open Setup Interface

If the button doesn't work, manually visit: http://192.168.4.1:5000

Step 3 Configure Your WiFi

In the setup interface, you'll:

  1. Scan for available WiFi networks
  2. Select your home network from the list
  3. Enter your WiFi password
  4. Click Connect

Your Mirror will connect to your WiFi and display a success message. You can then close the browser window.

‚úì Setup Complete! Your Mirror will now start in normal mode and be accessible on your home network.

After Setup

Once your Mirror is connected to WiFi:

  1. The setup interface will close automatically
  2. Your Mirror will finish booting in normal mode
  3. You can access your Mirror on your home WiFi network
  4. Your WiFi settings are saved for future boots

If you need to reconfigure WiFi in the future, there's an option in your Mirror settings to re-run setup mode.
